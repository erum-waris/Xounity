About Me Syed Qasim Ali Shah has been selected Member KP Assembly for the First time in 2024 General Elections He holds Masters in Economics degree and a businessman by profession He belongs to the prominent political family of Peshawar and the son of Ex Minister Syed Ali Shah

Political career

He entered politics as a youth member of the Pakistan Tehreek e Insaf (PTI) and became Secretary General of the PTI Youth Wing in 2013 [1] He contested in the 2015 Khyber Pakhtunkwa local elections [1] He contested in the 2024 general elections as a Pakistan Tehreek e Insaf backed Independent candidate from PK 81 Peshawar X[3] He won and secured 44310 votes while the runner up was Arslan Khan Nazim of ANP who secured 8177 votes [4] He was then made Provincial Health Minister for Khyber Pakhtunkhwa in the Gandapur ministry As Provincial Health Minister for Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Syed Qasim Ali Shah stated that he wanted to bring professionalism and innovation to the healthcare sector of the province He also stated that healthcare and education were top priorities of the ministry to face challenges[2] He notably visited the Khyber Medical University where he handed out scholarships and praised the university for strides in physiotherapy nursing, and health sciences programs He also supported the creation of more of the university s campuses in merged districts [2] Under Qasim the expansion of the Sehat Insaf Card was prioritized The Health Department under Syed Qasim Ali Shah announced a program aiming to expand free healthcare coverage under the Sehat Insaf Card and Ehsaas Programmes to 1 million people [5] He was reshuffled to the portfolio of Minister for Social Welfare and Women Empowerment in September 2024
